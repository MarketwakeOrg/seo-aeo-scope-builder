Appendix / Deliverable Definitions

What every line on the scope actually means.

A plain-language reference for every deliverable in this builder. Use it to walk a client through the scope without ambiguity.

3-month SEO + AEO content strategy
A rolling quarterly plan that maps content production (briefs, authored blogs, refreshes) against the keyword hitlist and AEO prompt list. Updated every quarter. Included in every package.
AEO prompt tracking
Ongoing tracking of the prompts the client's target buyers are actually asking Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. Every package includes a baseline total of tracked prompts: 25 for Current, 100 for High Tide, and 200 for Rip Tide. The foundation for all AEO optimization work.
AEO Tier 1 · SignalBundled with Current
GA4 organic traffic, event completion tracking, and LLM source traffic (visits referred by AI assistants). No Scrunch tooling. Bundled into the Current package at no extra cost.
AEO Tier 2 · PulseBundled with High Tide
Tier 1 + 100 prompts / mo tracked in Scrunch across Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. Includes a monthly AEO findings report with brand mention trends. Bundled into the High Tide package at no extra cost; $750 / mo delta if upgraded from Current.
AEO Tier 3 · SonarBundled with Rip Tide
Tier 2 + 200 prompts tracked. Adds share-of-voice analysis, trend tracking, and AEO gap analysis against competitors. Monthly findings report included. Bundled into the Rip Tide package at no extra cost; $1,100 / mo list price as an upgrade.
AEO Tier 4 · Horizon
Tier 3 + 300 prompts. Full citation source map, multi-model comparison analysis, and QBR-ready AEO data. Monthly findings report included. $1,600 / mo list price. Available as an upgrade on any package; the cost billed is the delta above what the package already bundles.
Authored blogs (Marketwake writes)Always an add-on
Fully researched, written, and edited blog posts delivered ready to publish. Same 4 standard + 1 long-form cadence per 5 posts. Authoring is never included in any package - it is always an upsell add-on.
Blog briefs
Every brief is optimized for both SEO and AEO: structured to rank in traditional search and to be cited by AI assistants (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity). Each brief includes target keyword, search intent, AEO prompt alignment, outline, internal links, and word count. Every 5 briefs follows a 4 standard (800 to 2k words) + 1 long-form (2 to 4k words) cadence.
Blog upload + publish
Marketwake handles the full publish workflow in the client's CMS: formatting, metadata, internal linking, image optimization, and scheduling. Client only needs to approve. Priced by total blog output per month: $600 for 1 to 10 blogs, $1,100 for 11 to 20 blogs, $1,600 for 21 to 30 blogs.
Custom GA4 events
Configuration of event tracking in Google Analytics 4 for the conversions and micro-conversions that actually matter to the client's business. Every package includes 10 custom events baseline. Rip Tide bundles 10 additional events (20 total). Need more? Add them as a one-time configuration fee with volume breaks starting at $200 / event.
Dedicated Marketwake strategistRip Tide only
A named senior strategist as the client's primary point of contact, leading quarterly planning and mid-cycle strategic shifts. Not available on Current or High Tide.
Dev hours · Technical SEO fixes10 hours bundled with Rip Tide
A dedicated monthly bucket of developer time used to implement the technical fixes surfaced by the Lumar analysis: schema markup, redirects, canonical tags, site speed, crawl errors, and indexation issues. Sold in 10, 20, or 30 hour buckets with a volume discount ($225 to $190 per hour). Rip Tide bundles 10 hours per month at no extra cost.
Keyword hitlist
A prioritized list of target keywords the client should rank for, scored by search volume, ranking difficulty, and commercial intent. 100, 150, or 200 keywords depending on package.
Monthly exec summary
A 1-page written summary delivered alongside the dashboard each month: what moved, why it moved, and what Marketwake is doing about it next month.
Monthly Looker Studio dashboard
A live Looker Studio dashboard with organic traffic, keyword rankings, conversion tracking, and AEO signals. Delivered with a written executive summary each month.
On-page / product-page optimizations
Monthly updates to existing pages: copy, headings, meta tags, schema, internal linking, and structure. For ecommerce brands this includes product detail pages. Design and dev time is billed separately or through the Dev Hours add-on.
Quarterly business review (QBR)
A live quarterly deep-dive presentation covering SEO + AEO performance, insights, recommendations, and findings. Delivered by the Marketwake strategy team. $3,500 per quarter, or included with Rip Tide.
Quarterly content strategy refresh
End-of-quarter review of what ranked, what didn't, and where to pivot the next 3 months of content production. Included in High Tide and Rip Tide.
Repurposed content
Refreshing an existing post counts as 0.5 of a content credit. Used to stretch output by mixing new and repurposed work when the strategy calls for it.
SEO audit
One-time baseline assessment of the site's organic health: indexation, site structure, internal linking, content gaps, and priority fix list. Delivered in the first 30 days of the engagement.
Technical SEO analysis + Lumar dashboardIncluded in all packages
Ongoing technical monitoring via Lumar (formerly DeepCrawl). Client gets read access to the dashboard for real-time visibility into crawl errors, broken links, site speed, schema issues, and indexation status.
Yext + review management
Marketwake manages the client's Yext listings across 200+ directories (Google, Bing, Apple Maps, Yelp, industry verticals) and handles review monitoring and response at every location. Priced per location with volume breaks starting at $185 / location, dropping $5 every 10 locations, floor of $100 / location, max 200 locations.
